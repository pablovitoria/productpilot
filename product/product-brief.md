# Product Brief

## ProductPilot

ProductPilot is an AI-assisted product discovery workspace that helps Product Managers transform customer feedback into confident product decisions through a structured, human-in-the-loop workflow.

## Problem

Product Managers receive customer feedback from many different sources: interviews, app reviews, surveys, support tickets, sales conversations, internal Slack messages, and online communities.

The challenge is not just collecting this feedback. The harder part is turning messy qualitative input into clear product opportunities, validating those opportunities with business context and product data, and communicating the reasoning behind product decisions.

AI can summarize and organize information, but great product decisions still require customer empathy, business context, quantitative evidence, strategic judgment, and trade-off thinking.

## Target User

The primary user is a Product Manager working on a digital product who needs to synthesize customer feedback, evaluate potential opportunities, and decide which problems are worth solving.

## Product Philosophy

AI should help Product Managers think faster, not think for them.

ProductPilot is designed as a human-in-the-loop workflow. AI accelerates synthesis, organization, and first drafts, while the Product Manager remains responsible for validating evidence, incorporating business context and product data, challenging assumptions, and making the final decision.

## Goals

- Reduce the time needed to synthesize customer feedback.
- Help Product Managers identify recurring themes and potential opportunities.
- Connect product opportunities back to customer evidence.
- Combine qualitative customer insights with quantitative product data.
- Guide Product Managers through a structured decision-making process.
- Generate reusable product artifacts that support product discovery and planning.

## Non-Goals

ProductPilot is not intended to be:

- A Jira replacement.
- A roadmap automation tool.
- A project management platform.
- A tool that makes product decisions automatically.
- A fully autonomous AI Product Manager.

## MVP Scope

The first version focuses on a single end-to-end product discovery workflow:

**Customer Feedback → AI Analysis → Potential Opportunity Identification → PM Decision Workspace → AI Challenge → Output Selection**

The MVP enables a Product Manager to paste customer feedback, receive AI-generated insights and potential opportunities, select the opportunity they want to explore, enrich the analysis with relevant product data and business context (such as adoption, conversion, retention, customer segments, or other key metrics), validate and refine the opportunity through a guided decision-making process, respond to AI-generated challenges, and generate the product artifact that best fits the next stage of the product lifecycle.

Available outputs include:

- Product Decision Canvas
- Epic Draft
- PRD Draft

## Success Criteria

The MVP is successful if a Product Manager can move from unstructured customer feedback to a validated product decision in less than 10 minutes while maintaining confidence that the final output reflects their own judgment—not simply an AI-generated recommendation.

## Why This Project Exists

Throughout my product work and graduate studies, I found myself repeatedly performing the same manual workflow: reviewing customer feedback, identifying recurring themes, validating ideas with product data, documenting opportunities, and preparing product recommendations.

While AI can dramatically accelerate parts of this process, I realized that the most valuable part of product management isn't generating ideas—it's evaluating them.

ProductPilot was created to explore how AI can accelerate product discovery while preserving the structured thinking, critical judgment, and business context that define great Product Managers.
