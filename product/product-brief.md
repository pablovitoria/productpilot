# Product Brief

## ProductPilot
ProductPilot is an AI-assisted product discovery workspace that helps Product Managers turn customer feedback into structured product opportunities while keeping human judgment at the center of decision-making.

## Problem

Product Managers receive customer feedback from many different sources: interviews, app reviews, surveys, support tickets, sales conversations, internal Slack messages, and online communities.

The challenge is not just collecting this feedback. The harder part is turning messy qualitative input into clear product opportunities, deciding what matters, and communicating the reasoning behind those decisions.

AI can help summarize and organize information, but product decisions still require customer empathy, business context, strategic judgment, and trade-off thinking.

## Target User

The primary user is a Product Manager working on a digital product who needs to synthesize customer feedback and decide which problems are worth exploring.

## Product Philosophy

AI should help Product Managers think faster, not think for them.

ProductPilot is designed as a human-in-the-loop workflow. AI helps with synthesis, structure, and first drafts, while the Product Manager remains responsible for prioritization, judgment, and final decisions.

## Goals

- Reduce the time needed to synthesize customer feedback.
- Help Product Managers identify recurring themes and pain points.
- Connect product opportunities back to customer evidence.
- Support better product decisions through structured thinking.
- Generate useful product artifacts such as problem statements, success metrics, and experiment ideas.

## Non-Goals

ProductPilot is not intended to be:

- A Jira replacement.
- A roadmap automation tool.
- A project management platform.
- A tool that makes product decisions automatically.
- A fully autonomous AI Product Manager.

## MVP Scope

The first version will focus on one core workflow:

**Customer feedback → AI synthesis → PM evaluation → Product Opportunity Brief**

The MVP will allow a Product Manager to paste customer feedback, receive AI-generated themes and opportunities, enrich those insights with relevant product data and business context (such as adoption, conversion, retention, customer segments, or other key metrics), validate the recommendations using their own judgment, and generate a concise Product Opportunity Brief.

## Success Criteria

The MVP is successful if a Product Manager can use it to move from messy feedback to a structured product opportunity in less than 10 minutes, while still feeling in control of the final decision.

## Why This Project Exists

I built ProductPilot to explore how AI can improve the product management workflow without removing the judgment that makes product management valuable.

The goal is not to automate product thinking. The goal is to make the early stages of discovery faster, clearer, and more evidence-based.
