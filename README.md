# ProductPilot

ProductPilot is an AI-assisted product discovery workspace that helps Product Managers turn unstructured customer feedback into evidence-backed product decisions.

Instead of asking AI to make product decisions, ProductPilot uses a human-in-the-loop workflow: **AI proposes. Product Managers decide.**

> 🚧 Active MVP under development.

## The Problem

Product Managers receive feedback from interviews, reviews, surveys, support tickets, and internal teams.

The challenge is turning those qualitative signals into structured opportunities, connecting them to relevant product data, and clearly documenting the reasoning behind a decision.

## Product Workflow

**Customer Feedback → AI Analysis → Opportunity Selection → PM Decision Workspace → AI Challenge → Product Artifact**

The experience is intentionally guided rather than chatbot-based.

## MVP

ProductPilot enables a Product Manager to:

- Paste unstructured customer feedback.
- Review and edit AI-generated synthesis.
- Understand how AI-generated insights were reached.
- Evaluate potential product opportunities.
- Add customer evidence, product metrics, and business context.
- Challenge assumptions before making a decision.
- Generate a Product Decision Canvas, Epic Draft, or PRD Draft.

## Success Measure

The MVP aims to reduce the average time from unstructured customer feedback to a structured product decision by at least **50%**.

## Why I Built It

I built ProductPilot to explore a question I have repeatedly encountered in product work:

**How can AI make product discovery faster and more evidence-based without replacing Product Manager judgment?**

This repository documents the journey from product definition and workflow design through implementation and iteration.
